✨ Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

A complete password management solution built with modern web technologies and a focus on privacy-first design.

🔐

End-to-End Encryption

Every password, username and note is encrypted with AES-256-GCM locally before it ever reaches our database. Zero-knowledge by design.

☁️

Real-Time Cloud Sync

Changes sync instantly across all your devices via Firebase Firestore. Your vault is always up-to-date everywhere you need it.

🚀

One-Click Launch

Open any saved website instantly with a single click. The "Launch" grid view shows all your sites as beautiful tiles for quick access.

🎨

Light, Dark & Auto Theme

Choose Light, Dark, or System mode that automatically follows your device's appearance setting for a comfortable experience.

🛡️

Security Alert System

If someone tries to access your vault from a different account, VaultKey detects it, blocks the attempt, and alerts you immediately.

📋

Full Audit Log

Every action — copy, launch, edit, delete — is logged with a timestamp so you always know exactly what happened in your vault.

🔒 Privacy First

Your data belongs
to you. Always.

VaultKey operates on a strict zero-knowledge model. We have designed the system so that it is technically impossible for us to access your passwords.

🛡️

No Password Storage

Passwords are encrypted client-side. Only the encrypted ciphertext reaches our servers.

🚫

No Ads. No Tracking.

We do not show ads, sell data, or use trackers. Your data is not our product.

📧

Security Alerts

We only contact you if we detect suspicious login activity on your account.
